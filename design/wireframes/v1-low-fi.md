# Food Sense V1 Low-Fidelity Wireframes

Version: V0.1
Date: 2026-06-14

These are text wireframes for the first mobile PWA structure. They are intentionally low-fidelity and focus on hierarchy, flow, and copy boundaries.

## 12. Design Notes for Next Step

- Keep the home screen focused on the three primary actions.
- Do not use a bottom `+` in V1.
- Merge `发现` into `档案` as the user's personal food reaction profile.
- Keep every AI upload moment explicit.
- Use gentle, non-diagnostic language in result and discovery screens.
- Make complex meal logging feel acceptable even with incomplete detail.
- Keep privacy controls reachable from the home screen and settings area.
